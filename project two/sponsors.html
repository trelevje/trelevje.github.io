Welcome To The Movement!

Thanks to our generous sponsors and partners, GEF is able to provide high quality sustainability programs, lessons, and contests to students, educators, and youth groups around the world. Sustainability Education is gaining traction in local, national, and international arenas as it becomes increasingly linked to sustainable development. By incorporating sustainability principles in all facets of education, from elementary-level audits to hands-on skills training for career and technical education, students are equipped with a unique, holistic perspective useful in any discipline.



Our Goal

GEF is a non-profit organization established in 2008 that focuses on developing and delivering sustainability education to students, educators, and adults globally. Find out more about GEF!

Become a Sponsor

GEF Sponsors and Partners contribute to a number of initiatives including course sponsorship for local educators, promotional campaigns, sustainability programs, and volunteer outreach. Free Sustainability Programs

GEF offers free programs as part of National Green Week to get your students excited about sustainability! National Green Week takes place from the first week of February through the end EcoChallengesIconof Earth Month (April). Choose a day or week to be your green week and select a sustainability theme to focus on from the list below. Themes include contests, lessons, activities, and more!

Waste Reduction Challenge- the largest waste reduction campaign in history!

Green Energy Challenge- make small behavior changes to reduce your energy consumption!

Green Thumb Challenge- connect kids with nature through gardening!

I Ride Green-change the way you travel to be more sustainable!

Sustainable Water Challenge- teaching students about the importance of water and sharing conservation and waste reduction strategies.

Green Building Program- educating students on the principles and strategies around green building
