Online and Classroom Based Sustainability

You and your students are invited to learn more about the important concepts of sustainability and green building with application based courses from GEF Institute. The Institute's online course platform takes the online educational experience to the next level. Enjoy video narrations, assessments, discussion forums, and activities as you work through the basic concepts of sustainability. High schools are encouraged to explore the Institute's Green Building curriculum, a fully scripted set of lessons that can be implemented through an online blended learning platform or as strictly classroom based education.

Free Sustainability Programs for National Green Week

GEF offers free programs to get your students excited about sustainability! Schools, classrooms and youth groups are encouraged to pick a theme for National Green Week to make a difference! All sustainability themed programs include free lessons, activities, contests and more!



Free Stand Alone Sustainability Lessons

GEF's Sustainability Lesson Clearinghouse contains a plethora of great lessons that can easily be integrated into existing curriculum. Search the database by grade level and subject area to find the perfect lesson or activity for your class!
